## Instructions

1. 1. To make the air fryer salmon tacos: Toss the salmon cubes with soy sauce, 2 tablespoons honey, garlic powder, olive oil, black pepper, 1 teaspoon Sriracha, and 1 teaspoon adobo seasoning. Allow marinating while your air fryer is preheating in air fry mode – 400ºF (200ºC).
2. 2. Put the salmon cubes in the air fryer drawer and cook for 4-5 minutes, tossing the cubes at mid-time so they cook evenly.
3. 3. In the meantime, prepare the sauce: in a bowl, combine mayonnaise with apple cider vinegar, 1 teaspoon of adobo seasoning, a dash of Sriracha, and cayenne (if using). Heat up the corn tortillas following the directions on the package.
4. 4. Spread the sauce on the tortillas and arrange cooked salmon cubes on top. Garnish with sliced avocado, feta cheese, red onion, cheese, and cilantro. Serve the air fryer salmon tacos immediately. Enjoy! ❤️
